**Search Product**
----
  Search product list. Accesible to users, admins and 3rd parties.

* **URL**

  /products/search

* **Method:**

  `GET`
  
*  **URL Params**

  **Required:**
 
   `name=[string]`
   `page=[integer]`

  **Optional:**
 
   `page=[integer]`
   

* **Data Params**

  None
